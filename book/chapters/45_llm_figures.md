## How to use these figures

- When you read a transformer block, map each box to one of two functions:
  - Mixing tokens (attention)
  - Transforming features (MLP/feedforward)
- When something feels abstract, point to the figure and answer:
  - What are the inputs/outputs of this block?
  - What information can flow between tokens here?
  - What is learned vs what is provided at inference time?
